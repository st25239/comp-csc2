Ingredients

2 cups Edmonds Self Raising Flour (300g) ⅛ tsp salt 90g butter, cubed (or Olivani Spread) ⅔ cup Meadow Fresh Original Milk (160ml) Filling 120g butter, softened (or Olivani Spread) ¼ cup Chelsea Soft Brown Sugar (50g) 2 tsp Chelsea White Sugar 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon Icing 1 cup Chelsea Icing Sugar (150g) 1 ½ - 2 Tbsp boiling water

Method

1 Preheat oven to 180°C bake / 160°C fan bake. Take 2 x 6-hole Texas muffin tins and grease 8 holes (the mixture should make 8 scrolls). 2 Dough Sift flour and salt into a medium-large bowl. Add the butter and rub in using your fingertips. 3 Make a well in the centre and add most of the milk. Mix gently to form a soft dough, adding the rest of the milk if necessary. 4 Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth. Roll dough out to a 25cm x 40cm rectangle, approximately 0.5cm thick. 5 Filling Using an electric mixer, beat butter, Chelsea Soft Brown Sugar, Chelsea White Sugar and ground cinnamon until light and fluffy. Spread evenly over the rolled out dough. 6 Roll dough up tightly from the long side. Use your hands to gently elongate the roll a little, if it needs evening out. Using a sharp knife, slice dough into 3cm rounds. 7 Place rounds in prepared tins. Cook for 25 minutes, until well risen and golden brown. 8 Icing Place Chelsea Icing Sugar and 1 1/2 tablespoons boiling water in a small bowl. Mix until smooth, adding extra water if needed to form a drizzling consistency. Drizzle icing over hot scrolls. 9 Best served warm! Scrolls keep well for 2-3 days and can be reheated in the oven or microwave.
